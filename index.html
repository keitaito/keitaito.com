Product

High5!

High5! is an arcade style iOS game about high five-ing.
There are high five, fist bump, and peace sign objects falling down on the screen. The player taps or swipes all high fives without touching the other objects. Based on zone points and combos, the player can gather points and try to get a high score! Download it here!
