Product

High5!

High5! is an arcade style iOS game about high five-ing.
There are high five, fist bump and peace sign objects falling down on the screen. The play taps or swipes all high fives and don't tap others. When the player taps a high five, the player gets points based on zone points and combo bonus. Try to get high score! Download it here!
